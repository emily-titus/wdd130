Mushroom Pattern

Mushroom:
Abbreviations used in this pattern:
FO – Fasten off
MR- Magic Ring
dec – Decrease
inc – Increase
sc – Single crochet
sl st – Slip stitch
st – Stitch
ch – Chain

Materials:
Red size 6 yarn
White size 6 yarn
5 mm crochet hook
Poly-fil

Mushroom steps:
Mushroom top:
1. Starting with red yarn, sc 6 in MR (6)
2. Sc around (6)
3. Inc around (12)
4. Sc around (12)
5. (Sc, inc) x 6 (18)
6. (Sc 2, inc) x 9 (24)
7. (Sc 3, inc) x 12 (30)
8. Sl st and fasten off, leave a long tail for sewing.

Mushroom stem:
1. With white, sc 6 in MR (6)
2. Inc around (12)
3. For rounds 3-6, sc around (12)
7. Dec around (6)
8. For rounds 8-11, sc around (6)
12. Inc around (12)
13. (Sc, inc) x 6 (18)
14. (Sc 2, inc) x 9 (24)
15. Sl st and fasten off, leave a tail
to sew the spots.
Use the red tail to sew together the stem
and top into the back loops,
stuff before it is fully closed.
Weave in the end and cut.
Use the white tail to weave through the red
top to make spots. Secure the tail.
[You can also use any variety of yarn to make
these cute mushrooms!]
